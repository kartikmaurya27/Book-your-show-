Founder's Journey

Parth Sharma –
Master Illusionist

It started with a borrowed deck of cards at age nine. Young Parth, growing up in Delhi, was captivated by the impossible — and spent every evening practising sleight-of-hand in front of a cracked mirror until the moves became second nature.

After formal training under legendary mentalist Rajan Kumar and a year-long apprenticeship in Mumbai's entertainment circuit, Parth launched his solo career in 2012. What began as weekend birthday shows quickly evolved into full-scale corporate spectacles and grand wedding productions.

Today, with over 1,200 shows performed across 40+ cities and international appearances in Dubai and Singapore, Parth Sharma is not just a magician — he is a storyteller of the impossible.
